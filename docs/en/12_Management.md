# 12. Management

> Define people, budget, timeline, and risk from a project management perspective, including long-term operational planning.

## 12.1 Team & Roles

- **Organization Chart:** Departments (Design, Engineering, Art, QA, Operations, Data).
- **Key Stakeholders:** List of System Owners and Leads.
- **Outsourcing & Partners:** Scope for Content, Art, Audio, and Technical Services.

## 12.2 Detailed Schedule

- **Milestones:** Prototype, Alpha, Beta, Soft Launch, Global Launch.
- **Deliverables:** Playable content, technical stability targets, and data goals per milestone.
- **Version Cadence:** Thematic planning for major updates (referencing industry standards like Genshin Impact or Tencent's live service models).

## 12.3 Budget

- **Personnel:** Costs broken down by role and development phase.
- **Procurement & Services:** Engines, SDKs, Cloud Services, CDN, Monitoring tools.
- **Marketing & UA:** Ad spend, User Acquisition, Branding campaigns.
- **LiveOps Budget:** Funds for events, new assets, and ongoing content production.

## 12.4 Risk Analysis

- **Design Risks:** Unproven mechanics, numerical balance issues.
- **Technical Risks:** Performance bottlenecks, network stability, platform certification.
- **Business Risks:** Market competition, policy changes, platform rule shifts.
- **Organizational Risks:** Key personnel turnover, cross-team collaboration friction.
- **Mitigation:** Probability, Impact, and Response Plan for each risk.

## 12.5 Localization Plan

- **Scope:** Target languages and regions.
- **Coverage:** Text, Voice, UI adaptation.
- **Compliance:** Culturalization (religion, symbols, legal restrictions).
- **Workflow:** Batching, Lock dates, and Regression testing for localized assets.

## 12.6 Test Plan

- **Types:** Functional, Performance, Compatibility, UX, A/B Testing.
- **Phases:** Tech Test, Closed Beta (CBT), Open Beta (OBT), Launch.
- **Bug Management:** Triage process and priority definitions.
- **Feedback Loop:** Collection methods (Community, CS, In-game reporting).

## 12.7 LiveOps & Events

> Plan the operational rhythm early, referencing best practices from long-term service games.

- **Event Types:** Holiday events, Version events, Permanent recurring systems.
- **Cadence:** Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Major Version cycles.
- **KPIs:** Acquisition, Reactivation, DAU, Monetization (ARPPU), Sink efficiency.
- **Pipeline:** Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) from concept to live deployment.

## 12.8 Data-driven Iteration

- **Linkage:** Integration with metrics defined in Sections 3.9 and 9.9.
- **Evaluation:** Post-launch review cycles and data-informed decision making.
- **Kill Switch:** Criteria for sunsetting or reworking underperforming systems.

## 12.9 GaaS Service Blueprint

> Long-term planning for Game as a Service.

- **Seasonal Calendar:**
    - Season duration and theme rotation logic.
- **LiveOps Cadence:**
    - Standard templates for Daily, Weekly, and Monthly activities.
- **Community Engagement:**
    - Events, KOL/Influencer collaboration, UGC incentive programs.
- **Crisis Management:**
    - SOP for server outages, critical bugs, and PR crises.

## 12.10 Retention Strategy (3B Model Application)

- **Day 1-7 Goals:**
    - Applying the 3B Model (Section 3.11) to retention interventions (Push notifications, Gifts, Recall campaigns).
- **Long-term Retention:**
    - Designing social bonds and sunk cost mechanisms.

## 12.11 Marketing & Landing Page Assets

- **Landing Page Requirements:**
    - Visualizing Unique Selling Points (USPs).
    - Funnel design (CTA placement, Lead magnets).
- **Creative Assets:**
    - Specs and direction for Ad creatives (Video, Static).
    - App Store Screenshots and Preview Video scripts.

## 12.12 User Research Methodology (OST)

- **Opportunity Solution Tree (OST):**
    - Collecting user Pain Points.
    - Transforming Pain Points into Opportunities and validating via Solutions (Experiments).
- **Feedback Loop:**
    - Community Feedback → Dev Adjustment → Public Announcement.

## 12.13 Success Metrics for Serious Games (Category Specific)

- **Behavioral Change Metrics:**
    - Habit formation (e.g., streak retention, real-life application).
- **Learning Outcomes:**
    - Quantifiable assessment of knowledge or skill acquisition.
